Bolognese Sauce

One of my boyfriend's favorite foods, this tomato sauce with ground beef is a game-changer to any pasta dish

An easy to make recipe filled with flavor to level up any pasta dish you want, and is the first of this list of recipes that I know my boyfriend loves

Ingredients:

  • salt and pepper
  • ground beef: 1KG
  • 1 big carrot, peeled and shredded
  • 1 big onion cut in small dices
  • 2 green and/or red bell pepper cut in very small dices
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • a pinch of thyme
  • two cups of tomato puree
  • two spoons of vegetal oil

Steps:

  1. Start with a pot with oil on high heat, when hot add the peppers and onions and cook until golden.
    Then add the chicken stock cubes and mix
  2. After adding the chicken stock add the ground beef, salt and pepper
  3. Keep mixing until the beef is done, lower the heat and then add the carrot, the tomato paste and thyme
    (you could also add a bay leaf). Now let the sauce cook and reduce in volume so all the flavor is more concentrated
  4. Let the sauce rest for about 10 minutes and then put it on top of your favorite pasta!
